## Why E-commerce Teams Need This Workflow

In e-commerce, creative velocity and clarity of selling points directly impact CTR and conversion. Nano Banana 2 helps teams shorten the path from idea to deployable visuals.

![Nano Banana 2 e-commerce selling point visual](https://img.limaxai.com/aiweb/limaxai-blog-1.png)

## Practical Workflow

### Step 1: One visual, one objective
- Hero image: capture attention
- Selling-point image: communicate one core benefit
- Detail image: reduce decision friction

### Step 2: Prompt structure = Product + Scene + Audience + Outcome

| Module | Example |
|---|---|
| Product | Kids posture corrector, full product visibility |
| Scene | Clean desk study setup, natural daylight |
| Audience | 7-10 years old child with parent context |
| Outcome | Clear support, comfort, and breathable wearing |

```text
E-commerce product highlight image, kids posture corrector in clean desk study scene, natural daylight, soft blue color palette, focus on ergonomic back support and comfort, close-up detail of breathable material, high-conversion product marketing style, Chinese e-commerce visual, ultra clear
```

### Step 3: Generate variants for fast A/B testing
Produce at least 3 variants for each selling point by changing composition, text placement, and style tone.
