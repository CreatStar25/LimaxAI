## 1. Visual traits and use cases

- **Minimal**: fewer elements, clear layers—wallpapers, covers, calm brands.
- **Sky mirror**: symmetrical reflections, soft horizon—serene mood.
- **Healing feel**: low saturation, soft light, airy tone—avoid harsh contrast.

## 2. Midjourney prompt structure

**Scene + weather/light + water/mirror + style + quality**. Example:

```text
Minimalist healing landscape, sky mirror reflection, calm lake, soft pastel sky, symmetrical composition, dreamy atmosphere, ultra clean, fine art photography style --ar 16:9
```

| Control | Prompt hint |
|---------|-------------|
| Symmetry | symmetrical, mirror reflection, perfect horizon |
| Soft light | soft light, golden hour or blue hour |
| Minimal | minimal elements, negative space |

## 3. Iteration tips

1. Fix **aspect ratio** and **horizon position** before color tweaks.
2. If too literal, add soft words: soft, hazy, ethereal.
3. Keep 2–3 style keywords fixed for a consistent series.
