## 1. Visual elements of Chinese-style autumn

- **Color**: maple red, ginkgo gold, restrained saturation in ink-wash mountains.
- **Motifs**: distant peaks, wild geese, pavilion corners—suggest, don’t overload.
- **Negative space**: reserve room for titles and seal-style layout.

## 2. Prompt structure

**Subject + Chinese painting/photo style + autumn cues + light + negative space + quality**. Example:

```text
Chinese style autumn poster, ink wash and golden ginkgo, misty mountains, elegant negative space for typography, soft diffused light, fine art poster layout, ultra detailed --ar 2:3
```

| Module | Note |
|--------|------|
| Style blend | ink painting texture, contemporary Chinese illustration |
| Autumn | maple red, golden leaves, misty river |
| Layout | poster layout, title safe area |

## 3. Poster workflow

1. **Sketch the hero** and confirm autumn palette and whitespace.
2. Refine **detail and sharpness**, then add typography.
3. Keep one fixed color and brushword set per series.

## 4. Conclusion

Chinese autumn posters rely on **motif + negative space + unified tone**. On **LimaxAI** with **Midjourney**, generate variants and select the best for final layout.
