## Why Cover Images Drive Traffic

Users decide to click before reading your article. Cover design directly affects CTR and first-session quality.

![Nano Banana blog cover example](https://img.limaxai.com/aiweb/limaxai-blog-2.png)

## Three Rules for High-CTR Covers
1. Instantly readable topic signal
2. Single visual focus
3. Mobile-friendly legibility

## Prompt Template

```text
High CTR blog cover, clean composition, strong focal point, bold readable title area, modern AI tutorial style, high contrast, social media friendly, professional tech color palette, ultra clear
```

## A/B Test Matrix

| Variable | Version A | Version B |
|---|---|---|
| Headline style | Direct value | Curiosity question |
| Main visual | Person close-up | Product/interface close-up |
| Color mood | Cool tech | Warm lifestyle |
